Rocket Boys

IMDb 8.9
2022 2 Seasons SonyLIV

Starring Jim Sarbh as Dr. Homi Bhabha and Ishwak Singh as Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, Rocket Boys is a fictionalized dramatization of how these two extraordinary men built India's nuclear and space programs from scratch.

The series beautifully blends science with human drama, showcasing their personal struggles, professional rivalries, and the political challenges they faced in post-independence India.

IMDb 8.7
2020 1.5 Seasons Disney+ Hotstar

Starring Kay Kay Menon as Himmat Singh, Special Ops is an edge-of-the-seat thriller based on real covert operations conducted by Indian intelligence agencies.
